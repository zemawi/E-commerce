Our Heritage

EthioLeather is a family-owned business that has been crafting premium leather goods in Ethiopia for over three generations. We combine traditional techniques with modern designs to create products that stand the test of time.

Our leather is sourced from local Ethiopian tanneries that practice sustainable and ethical production methods. Each piece is handcrafted by skilled artisans who take pride in their work.

When you purchase from EthioLeather, you're not just buying a product - you're supporting local craftsmanship and preserving cultural heritage.
